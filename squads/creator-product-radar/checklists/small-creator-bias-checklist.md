# Small Creator Bias Checklist

- [ ] Top-ranked products do not require a massive audience to demonstrate value.
- [ ] Ranking logic does not overreward premium production quality proxies.
- [ ] Accessibility scoring favors products that smaller creators can realistically test or film.
- [ ] Suggested hooks work for creators without brand exclusivity or warehouse inventory.
- [ ] At least part of the ranking explains why a product is viable for emerging creators.
